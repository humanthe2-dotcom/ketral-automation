Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Ketral Automation ("we," "us," or "our") provides automated missed call response and booking services for local businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our services and SMS outreach.

1. Information We Collect

  • Business name, phone number, and general category — sourced from publicly available business directories.
  • Replies and messages sent to us via SMS.
  • Booking information voluntarily provided by end customers of our clients (name, preferred time, reason for contact).

2. How We Use Information

  • To contact local business owners about our services via SMS.
  • To operate the missed call booking bot on behalf of our clients.
  • To deliver booking information to our clients' calendars.
  • To maintain records of opt-outs and ensure compliance with applicable messaging laws.

3. We Do Not Sell or Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information is used solely to provide and improve our services.

4. SMS Messaging

By engaging with our SMS messages, you acknowledge that standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of receiving messages from us at any time by replying STOP to any message. Once you opt out, we will not send further messages to your number.

5. Data Retention

We retain conversation records only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. Opted-out numbers are retained on a blocklist to ensure no further messages are sent.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data to the best of our ability.
